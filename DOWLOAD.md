# Download

PocketCloud consists of two apps — install the **Server app (C2)** on the old device you want to use as your personal cloud, and the **Client app (C1)** on the device you'll use to access your files.

⬇️ [Download PocketCloud](https://github.com/Tpskevin13/PocketCloud/releases)

## Tailscale
To access your files from outside your home network, install Tailscale on both devices.

⬇️ [Download Tailscale](https://tailscale.com/download/android)
